How the Snake Draft Works
Draft order: Worst Season 1 standing picks first. Order reverses each round — no team gets two consecutive picks.
Round 1: T6 → T5 → T4 → T3 → T2 → T1
Round 2: T1 → T2 → T3 → T4 → T5 → T6
Round 3: T6 → T5 → T4 → T3 → T2 → T1
...repeats for 6 rounds → 6 picks each = 36 players + 6 captains = 42 total
