✅ Option 1 — Pair Picks + Snake · Season 3
Pairs (bottom 2 / middle 2 / top 2) each pick their 1st and 2nd picks together as a block, then snake top-down from pick 13. Wingman must be picked by end of pick 5.
Picks 1–2: Anil · Pratik    (bottom pair, simultaneous)
Picks 3–4: Anil · Pratik    (2nd pick)
Picks 5–6: Harsha · Shanthan (middle pair, simultaneous)
Picks 7–8: Harsha · Shanthan (2nd pick)
Picks 9–10: Koti · Karthik   (top pair, simultaneous)
Picks 11–12: Koti · Karthik   (2nd pick)
Pick 13 ↓ snake: Karthik → Koti → Shanthan → Harsha → Pratik → Anil
...continues snake for remaining rounds
Wingman rule: Must be picked by end of pick 5.

Monthly Event Format — Option A ✅ Confirmed
✅ Option A — 6 RR + Ranked Finals · Season 3
6 Round Robin slots (each team plays exactly 4 games), followed by 5th/6th + 3rd/4th simultaneous, then Grand Final. 2:00–5:20 PM.
2:00 PM — Slot 1: T1 vs T2 | T3 vs T5
2:25 PM — Slot 2: T1 vs T3 | T4 vs T6
2:50 PM — Slot 3: T2 vs T4 | T5 vs T6
3:15 PM — Slot 4: T1 vs T5 | T2 vs T3
3:40 PM — Slot 5: T3 vs T4 | T1 vs T6
4:05 PM — Slot 6: T2 vs T6 | T4 vs T5
4:30 PM — 5th Place | 3rd Place (simultaneous)
4:55 PM — 🏆 Grand Final · #1 vs #2
5:20 PM — Complete
Scoring: Win = 2 pts · Loss = 0 · Score to 21 (win by 2) or cap at 25 · No draws.

Wingman System
Core members serve as Wingmen — they are drafted as regular players but provide team support. They are never captains. Each core member rotates as Wingman for different teams across seasons. Wingman must be picked by the captain before end of pick 5.
