Game Rules
12.1 Ball In / Out
Ball landing on line = IN. Ball fully outside = OUT.
12.2 Blocking
A block does NOT count as one of the 3 hits.
12.3 Overreach — FVL Rule
Before attack completes → hands off. After attack completes → fair game, even over net.
Attack = ball going toward opponent's side. Umpire has final discretion.
12.4 Violations
Foot fault · net touch · ball under net · carrying.
Serve can't be blocked or spiked. Double contact = foul.
Center line: violation if crosses AND interferes.
12.5 Rotation
Mandatory clockwise. Right-back serves first.
Back-row attacks only behind 10-foot line.
12.7–12.8 Replacement & Tiebreaker
Similar skill level · accepted by both captains.
Tiebreak: Wins → Points diff → Coin toss. No tiebreaker games.
