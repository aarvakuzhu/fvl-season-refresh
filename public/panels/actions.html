Qualifying Criteria
A team that wins 2 or fewer games in each of 2 consecutive months is identified as a bottom performer and may raise a Change Request (CR).
Example: 3 wins in Month 1 + 1 win in Month 2 = does NOT qualify (Month 1 > 2). Both months must individually be ≤2 wins.
CR Process
1.Qualifying team raises a CR. Swap can be requested with either of the Top 2 teams in season standings at that point.
2.30-minute discussion window — captains and wingmen of both teams. Held on the following week's practice game day after the second qualifying month.
3.Rolling every month thereafter based on the 2 most recent consecutive months.
Transfer Window eligibility is tracked automatically in the Season 3 → Transfer Window tab (core members only).
