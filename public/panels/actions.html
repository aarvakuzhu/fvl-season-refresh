📦 Archive — Previous Proposals (Not Adopted)
Nano Tier Proposal — Not proceeding

A 3-team entry tier (Nano) was proposed for Season 2. Teams would enter at Nano level with monthly promotion/relegation. Core members decided against this for Season 2 in favour of a simpler single-division 6-team structure.

Original proposal: 4 Mini teams + 3 Nano teams = 7 teams total. Monthly swap: Nano champion → Mini, Mini bottom team → Nano. May be revisited for Season 3.

Auction / IPL-Style Draft — Not proceeding

An auction draft was proposed where captains bid for players from equal budgets (100 units). RTM (Right to Match) tokens (1–3 per captain) would allow targeted retention.

Core members decided on snake draft instead for Season 2 due to simplicity. Auction format may be revisited in future seasons as the league matures.

Promotion / Relegation System — Not proceeding

A monthly P/R swap was proposed (bottom Mini team drops, Nano champion rises). Not applicable for Season 2 as the single-division format has no tier movement. May apply once Nano is introduced.
