Core members · Fixed roles

The Wingman System

🤝
4 Mini Wingmen
One per Mini team — permanently assigned. Advise captains, observe player development, flag tier changes. Co-sign post-draft trades. Mini only — not applicable to Major.
🌱
1 Nano Coordinator
Runs full Nano event. Schedules, scores, identifies monthly champion. Scouts Mini captain candidates. Recruits new players into FVL.
⚙️
1 Floating / League Manager
League-wide admin, dispute resolution, logistics. Backup for absent Wingman or Coordinator. Sunil fills this role for Season 1.
Core principle: Core members are never team captains. Their influence is through mentorship and institutional knowledge, not control. Roles are fixed — no rotation.

Season Transition — 4 Steps

1
Captain Selection
Core members select all 7 captains (4 Mini + 3 Nano). Captains auto-fill spot #1 — excluded from draft pool. Auction: captain value deducted from team budget before bidding.
2
Player Preference Declaration — 48-hour window
Mini-available → enters Mini draft (overflow goes to Nano). Nano-only → skips Mini draft entirely. Nano-only is a legitimate choice — respected without question.
3
Mini Auction Draft
4 Mini captains bid for 7 players each. Equal starting budget minus captain value. Tier balance enforced: max 1 Tier S, 1–2 Tier A, 2–3 Tier B per roster. Unsold player at min bid goes to nominating captain.
4
Nano Auction Draft
3 Nano captains draft from Nano pool (Nano-only + Mini overflow). Same auction format. Flexible tier balance — development focus.

🔄 Right to Match (RTM)

Recommended: 1 RTM per captain per draft.

When another captain bids on a player who was on your team last season, you may match the winning bid and retain them.

Why RTM: No prior-season record keeping. Live bidding sets the price. Self-correcting — rivals who overbid drain their own budget.

Parameters (pending vote): 1 RTM · 60-second window · Same tier only.

🐍 Snake Draft (Alternative)

Available as alternative to auction — see Appendix A in the full governance doc. Worst record picks first; order reverses each round. No budget needed. Core members vote to use for any given season.
