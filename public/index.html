Game Rules
⚖️ Referee's call is final. Only captains may address the referee during a game.
12.1 Scoring
Games to 21, rally scoring. 20-20 → 2-point lead wins. 24-24 → first to 25 wins.
3 hits per team. No player hits twice in succession. Ball on line = in play.
Serve behind baseline, hand only. Server joins play after serving.
12.2 Blocking
Defensive team may jump and block. A block does NOT count as one of the 3 hits.
12.3 Overreach & Attack — FVL Rule
Before attack is finished → hands off. After attack is finished → fair game to block, even over the net.
Attack = ball clearly travelling toward opponent's side — spike, pass, push, tip.
1st, 2nd, or 3rd contact — all fair once attack is complete. Umpire has final say.
12.4 Violations
Foot fault on serve · net touch · ball under net · carrying.
Serve cannot be blocked or spiked. Double contact = foul (except block+hit, or first single-action hit).
Center line: violation if body crosses AND interferes. Full body crossing = foul regardless.
12.5 Rotation
Mandatory clockwise. Right-back serves first. Rotate after winning rally and regaining serve.
Back-row may attack from behind 10-foot line. Cannot attack/block above net in front of it.
12.7–12.8 Replacement & Tiebreaker
Replacement: similar skill, not played that game or prior, accepted by both captains.
Tiebreaker order: 1. Wins   2. Points diff   3. Coin toss. No tiebreaker games played.

Right to Match (RTM)
When another captain bids on a player from your prior team, you may match the winning bid and retain them.
Why RTM over Keepers:
No prior-season record keeping needed. Live bidding sets the price — you simply decide if it's worth paying. Self-correcting: rivals who overbid drain their own budget.
Pending vote: 1 RTM per captain (budget pressure focus) or 3 RTM (chemistry focus)? 60-second decision window, same tier only.
Season Transition — 4-Step Draft Process
1
Captain Selection
All 7 captains selected (4 Mini + 3 Nano). Captains auto-fill spot #1 — excluded from pool. Auction: captain's value deducted from team budget before bidding begins.
2
Player Preference Declaration — 48 hours
Mini-available enters the Mini draft (overflow goes to Nano). Nano-only skips Mini entirely. Both choices are fully respected — no pressure either way.
3
Mini Auction Draft
4 Mini captains bid for 6 remaining spots each. Equal starting budget. Rotating nominations. Min bid 1 unit. Budget floor = 1 unit per remaining spot. Tier balance: max 1 Tier S + 1–2 Tier A + 2–3 Tier B per roster.
4
Nano Auction Draft
3 Nano captains draft from Nano-only pool + Mini overflow. Same auction format. Flexible tier balance with a development focus.
