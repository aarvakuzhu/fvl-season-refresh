Skip Rule Explained
Captains with a later "entry round" get bonus early picks as compensation.

Entry round N = picks in Round 1 to N-1, then skipped in Round N, then picks every round from N+1 onward.

Example: Harsha (entry R2) picks in Round 1, skipped in Round 2, picks from Round 3 onward.
Snake Order
Within each round's active captains, picks flow → left-to-right in odd rounds and ← right-to-left in even rounds.
Order within each round is based on captain position (set below).
